about me

hi! my name is jen and i’m a senior studying economics with a minor in east asian studies. i’m from northern va near dc. outside of class, i enjoy going to the gym, exploring boston with my friends, and listening to music. growing up, i loved arts and creating but i realized in recent years that i lost some of my creative outlets. i’ve been making a conscious effort to explore activities to exercise my creativity. for example, i’m really passionate about edm and am learning how to produce + dj as one of my artistic outlets! i wanted to take this course since i’m interested in product design and i want to make cute little trinkets. this was an amazing opportunity to get some hands-on experience, be creative, and it’s going to be so much fun!
