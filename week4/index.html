week 4

oh no no no!

for microcontroller week, i used a servo to make an oh no no no hand waving sign

the goal was to click a button to initiate the sequence, kind of like those staples “that was so easy” buttons. this servo was adapted from the final project that i had already made, but the inclusion of the button makes it more of a creative project.

I got the stl for the waving finger from thingiverse

download arduino code

Then, I played around with different acceleration times to get to a speed that seemed appropriate. I set it to wave at that speed for around 5 seconds, when it will stop. Then, to start the sequence again, you need to click the button once more.
