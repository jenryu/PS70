challenges and solutions

originally, the plan was for me to use a solenoid, which is what i did in my mvp. however, the problem is that the solenoid doesn't go up enough to click the pen mechanism the entirety of the way. instead, i pivoted to using a servor motor, where the extensions on the arms allow it to swing enough to hit the mechanism.

a lot of things broke. the gantry from the writing project was working earlier, but as i was rewiring things, the motor drivers fried, and there were no more left. this put me in a tough situation since this meant i couldnt get the gantry to work at all. however, in principal it would work, and i still formatted the servo motor arm to be able to reach the click-pen mechanism.
