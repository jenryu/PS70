project objective

create a box + fusion tutorial

miffy box

i wanted to add a fun twist to my box by drawing inspiration from miffy, the popular bunny character. using fusion, i designed a rectangular box with five panels and finger joints, measuring approximately 9.35 x 6 x 6 inches, similar in size to a shoebox. in order to attach bunny ears, i created a lid that would remain permanently open with some space allotted for ear slots.

during the CAD process, i encountered some issues with the lid, which didnâ€™t fit correctly. i noticed the error when i assembled the box in fusion, but i learned about a tool in office hours that could cut out pieces of the lid to fit the finger joints without manually drawing rectangular patterns like the other panels.

the laser cutting process went smoothly, though the box dimensions were too large for a single piece of cardboard. i removed one of the side panels of the box from the initial laser cutting to resolve the issue, cutting it independently and reattaching it back together during the assembly process with a hot glue gun. i added some finishing touches: a miffy face and a fluffy tail from some cotton pads!
